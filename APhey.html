Voting Tips & Guides

1. Verify Your Voter Registration

Ensure you are registered in the correct precinct and bring valid identification.

2. Read About the Candidates

Before voting, research the credentials and platforms of all candidates. Be an informed voter!

3. Be Organized on Election Day

Bring all necessary documents, know your polling place, and allow yourself ample time to vote.

4. Fill Out the Ballot Carefully

Follow the instructions on the ballot. Avoid erasures and mistakes that could invalidate your vote.

5. Vote for the Right Candidates

Make sure you understand the qualifications and platforms of the candidates you choose.
